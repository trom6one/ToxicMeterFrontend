TOXIC - Сlick to increase toxicity (1/s).

Common:
Click on any place in ToxicMeter for return to original size.
Hold on any place in ToxicMeter for move it.

Unsolvable problems:
Double or multi clicks trigger fullscreen opening stream window.

Temporal problems:
After dragging and dropping, the extension position
does not automatically adjust to the size of the window
and may hide behind the edge of the screen.
